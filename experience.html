EXPERIENCE

Hatyai, 27Sept-28Sept 2019

I was pleased with my Khlong Hae Floating Market visit. People are friendly, the cuisine is delicious, and the scenery is beautiful. Unfortunately, the Floating Market is only open from Friday to Sunday. So, if you're planning a trip to Hat Yai, try to incorporate one of those weekend days into your schedule to prevent missing out.

Pulau Langkawi, 27Feb-1 March 2020

We stayed in the Pentai Cenang area, which is a renowned tourist destination. There are beaches with white sand and emerald green sea. The Island Hopping Langkawi boat cruises are really inexpensive. In contrast to jet-ski trips, you will be aboard a boat with up to 20 other people. This permits tour firms to keep their prices affordable.
